    About Us

    IT, which stands for Information Technology, is a broad field that encompasses the management, development, and use of technology to store, transmit, and retrieve information. It involves the utilization of computer systems, software, networks, and telecommunications to solve various business and organizational challenges.

    The primary goal of IT is to facilitate the efficient management and communication of information within an organization. This involves tasks such as data storage, data processing, data analysis, network management, software development, system administration, cybersecurity, and technical support.

    Our Services

    We are provide various type of services.

    Software Development

    IT teams develop and maintain software applications to meet the specific needs of this organization. This involves designing, coding, testing, and deploying software solutions. We also ensure that software systems are updated and patched regularly.

    Network Management

    IT professionals manage and maintain the organization’s computer networks, including LANs, WANs, and the Internet. We configure network devices, monitor network performance, troubleshoot connectivity issues and ensure network security.

    Data Management

    IT teams handle the storage, organization, and security of data within this organization. We set up and maintain databases, implement backup and recovery procedures and ensure data integrity and privacy.

    Technical Support

    IT professionals provide technical assistance and support to users within the organization. We help troubleshoot hardware and software issues, assist with software installations and updates and resolve network connectivity problems.

    IP PBX Setup & Maintenance

    Our IP-PBX (Internet Protocol Private Branch Exchange) system has a physical hardware or a virtual server. We Create user accounts for employees and assign extensions for internal communication. This is allows seamless connectivity within our organization.

    Digital Attendence Maintenance

    Digital attendance systems automate the process of tracking attendance. This system is reduces and saves time for both administrators and attendees. We use biometric technologies such as fingerprint scanning, facial recognition for secure and accurate identification.

    CCTV Monitoring & Maintenance

    We Install CCTV cameras strategically to cover critical areas. blind spots and the specific security needs of the location. We Ensure that the CCTV system operates 24/7 without interruptions. Our Employee personnely monitor the live feed from cameras.

    Basic Computer Servicing

    Basic computer servicing involves routine maintenance and troubleshooting to keep a computer running smoothly. We provide the basic services of computers and IT devices under our health sector through IT division.
